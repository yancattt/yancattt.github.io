About Me

Hello! My name is Yan Li, and I am a second-year master’s student at Columbia University Mailman School of Public Health, focusing on Epidemiology. I am passionate about public health research, data science, and health education.

I have a background in Medical Laboratory Science and have worked as a Medical Laboratory Technologist in both the United States and China. Below, you’ll find more information about my experience, education, research projects, and skills.
